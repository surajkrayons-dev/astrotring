## What Is Rahu Dosh? The Complete Guide to the Planet of Illusion and Obsession

In **Vedic astrology (Jyotish Shastra)**, no planet generates more anxiety, confusion, or fascination than **Rahu**.

It is the planet of:

- Illusion
- Obsession
- Amplification
- Desire

When it creates challenges in your birth chart, the condition is known as **Rahu Dosh**.

Here is a powerful insight:

> 85% of people consult an astrologer during Rahu's Dasha or Antardasha.

When Rahu runs your life, the world feels magnetic — yet chaotic. Full of opportunity — yet foggy.

That is Rahu’s signature.

# What Is Rahu? Understanding the Shadow Planet

Rahu is one of the two **shadow planets (Chhaya Grahas)**.

It has:

- No physical body
- No mass
- No light of its own

It is smoke. Reflection. Illusion.

Rahu represents:

- Maya (illusion)
- Amplification
- Obsessive attachment
- Perpetual dissatisfaction

Rahu always creates:

**Attachment + Dissatisfaction**

You want it intensely.  
You get it.  
You still want more.

That is Rahu.
